About the Event

On November 28, 2024, Team Anterix successfully concluded its star-studded event, Anterstellar. The event began with an insightful lecture on space enigmas by Alok Mandavgane, Joint Secretary at Aryabhat Foundation, leaving the audience captivated.

The second session featured solar and lunar sightings, where students observed celestial wonders through a solar box and telescope. Enthralled, they watched as sir pointed out constellations, stars, planets and many more marvels of the night sky.

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to Alok Mandavgane sir for making this event a success!

  • Event Date

    28 th November , 2024

  • Event Schedule

    2:30pm - 4:00pm (Session - 1): Guest Speaker Session with Mr. Alok Mandavgane
    4:15pm - 7:15pm (Session - 2): Solar and Lunar Sighting

  • Event Goal

    Ignite curiosity about astronomy through expert insights and celestial observations.
    Expand knowledge with an engaging lecture and interactive skywatching.

    Session 2- Backside of Academic Block - 1
