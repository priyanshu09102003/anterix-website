About the Event

The universe is a vast, mysterious expanse, and it all began with the fundamentals of science, mathematics, and belief. To celebrate World Space Week (4th-10th October), Club ANTERiX proudly presents BASiX, its first mega event dedicated to pushing the limits of thought and discovery.

This event invites participants to engage in challenges that test their intelligence, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities. From scientific puzzles to thought-provoking scenarios, every challenge is designed to expand your perspective on space and beyond.
