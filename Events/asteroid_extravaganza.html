About the Event

Join Club Anterix on International Asteroid Day, 30th June 2023, for an out-of-this-world event! Immerse yourself in a two-session extravaganza: dive into celestial exploration with our captivating Astronomy with Python workshop, followed by a cosmic cinematic experience during our breathtaking documentary screening.

Expand your knowledge and ignite your passion for the universe. Don't miss this stellar opportunity!

  • Event Date

    30 th June , 2023
    International Asteroid Day

  • Event Schedule

    10:00am - 01:00pm (Session - 1): Python-Powered Astronomy
    2:00pm - 4:00pm (Session - 2): Cosmic Documentary

  • Event Goal

    Learn the use of Python in space and astronomy
    Expand knowledge with documentary screening.

  • Event Location

    Academic Block 1 and Auditorium 1
