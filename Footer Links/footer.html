FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Got questions? We've got answers! Explore our FAQs to learn more about Anterix, our events, and how you can be a part of our journey.

  • What is Anterix?

    • Anterix is a student-led club dedicated to space, aviation, and scientific exploration. We organize events, workshops, and hands-on projects to fuel curiosity and innovation.

  • Who can join Anterix?

    • Anyone with a passion for space, astronomy, aviation, or science can join! Whether you're a beginner or an expert, there's a place for you at Anterix.

  • What kind of events does Anterix organize?

    • We host stargazing nights, astronomy workshops, guest lectures, space tech discussions, and hands-on aerospace projects to engage members in learning and discovery.

  • How can I become a member?

    • You can join Anterix by filling our official recruitment forms that are circulated from time to time through official email of the institution or in social media handles/communities.

  • Do I need prior knowledge of space or aviation to join?

    • Not at all! Anterix welcomes enthusiasts of all levels. Our events and activities are designed to help everyone learn and grow.

  • How can I stay updated on club activities?

    • Follow us on our social media pages and check our official website for updates on upcoming events and projects.

  • Can I contribute ideas or organize events?

    • Yes! We encourage members to share ideas and take initiative in organizing events, discussions, and workshops.

  • Are there any membership fees?

    • Membership details, including any fees, are announced after the recruitment process. Membership fees consolidates your permanent position in the club.

  • How can I contact Anterix?

    • You can reach us via email, social media, or by attending our club meetups on campus.
